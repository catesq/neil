Connecting the Cross Delay (Effect)
Your screen should show now appear a bit like the following (screen arrangement does not matter):
Now all that is left to do is to connect these machines together and output them to the Master. To do this simply follow the steps you've already learned about connecting machines. First connect the PrimiFun to the X-Delay . Next connect the X-Delay to the Master .

If you did not stop the song before disconnecting the PrimiFun from the Master, you will hear the new chain's output immediately. If you did stop the song, press F5 to start it again.
You may now start changing the cross delays parameters by double-clicking on it to bring up its dialog box just as you had in the last screen.

Tip: If you end up with a machine sound you aren't happy with, you may reset it to its default parameters by selecting <Default> from the preset drop-down list located on the upper left of the parameter dialog window.
