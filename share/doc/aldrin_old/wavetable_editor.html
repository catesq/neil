Wavetable Editor
The Wavetable Editor in Aldrin allows you to add samples ( .WAV files) and Instruments ( .XI files) to your songs. These waveforms may then be accessed in any machine which supports the Wavetable Editor (trackers, etc.).

To access the Wavetable Editor press F9 .

In Aldrin, the term Wavetable refers to a collection of samples with specific attributes given to them (mainly loops and envelopes ), related to one song. Therefore each song contains its own Wavetable, which is composed of samples loaded from your hard-disk and saved into the .BMX files.

Note : To save your samples within a song (for distribution) you must save the file as a .BMX. A .BMW only saves paths to the files on your hard disk.
