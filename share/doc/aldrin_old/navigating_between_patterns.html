Navigating Between Patterns
As you create more Patterns for your machines, you will want to quickly navigate between them. There are currently two ways to accomplish this in Aldrin.

1. You may easily select the pattern by clicking the drop-down-box labeled Pattern on the top toolbar of the Pattern Editor.
2. In most cases a much faster way to switch between patterns is to use the + (plus) and - (minus) keys located on your keypad.
