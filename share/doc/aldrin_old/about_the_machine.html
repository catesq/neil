About the Machine Editor
The Machine Editor is the musical "workbench" of Buzz. To access the Machine Editor press F3 from anywhere within Aldrin, or select the Machine Editor Icon from the toolbar. It is in essence your "software studio". A good analogy would be to consider it a huge empty rack, waiting to be loaded with gear.

In Aldrin, each piece of gear referred to as a machine .

Once machines are added to the Machine Editor, you may connect (route) them in any way you want. The final output is always routed to the Master Machine in order to produce output to your audio card.
