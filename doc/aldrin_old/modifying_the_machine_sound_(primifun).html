Modifying the Machine Sound (PrimiFun)
Now that your melody is playing, you may change various parameters to alter the sound of the synth itself. To do this, switch to the Machine Editor ( Press F3 ) and double click on the PrimiFun machine . This should bring up the following parameter dialog window:
Changing the way a machine sounds is extremely easy. Just click on any handle horizontal bar and drag it either left or right. These horizontal bars are called parameters and control the various aspects of machines in Aldrin. Every machine has its own unique set of parameters, which may be tweaked in real-time , recorded in real-time , or scripted into the Pattern editor (in the various columns of periods you viewed a few minutes prior).
