Understanding Notes in Buzz
Notes in Aldrin operate exactly as they do in any other musical instance in the world. Aldrin supports 10 octaves of sound, starting at C-0, and ending at B-9. Aldrin also supports polyphony on a generator basis, allowing you to create create chords (see Tracks ) of synths and samples.

I will admit that this title may be somewhat misleading. It is far beyond the scope of this document to explain musical theory and notes to Aldrin users. There are plenty of great documents scattered throughout the web. Learning and understanding music theory will not only help you in Buzz, but in all music software. It is stronly suggested that you do so.
