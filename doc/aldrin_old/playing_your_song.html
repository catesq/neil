Playing your Song
Now that your song is created, simply press F5 to play it. You will notice that it will loop the 16 notes of the pattern you just entered. This is the default loop length in Aldrin. You may change this at any time.

Note: if your song is not looping, you must enable looping in the main toolbar by clicking the loop icon , to the right of the stop icon .

If you are still in the Sequence Editor ( F4 ) you will see a cursor moving along with the music, looping every 16 notes.

You may stop your song by pressing F8 at any time.
