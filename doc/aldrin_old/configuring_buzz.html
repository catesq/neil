Configuring Aldrin
Configuration within Aldrin is extremely simple. To configure Aldrin select Preferences from the view menu at the top of your screen. You will be presented with a window containing the following 4 tabs.
 
 
Important Note : Aldrin preferences are not saved until you hit the apply button (gray above). The apply button will become clickable whenever you have made a change. Click it at any time to save that change.
