Looping Samples

There are six steps to creating effective sample loops in Aldrin:

  1. Select a Sample from the Wavetable Samples

  2. Enable Looping by checking the Loop check-box above the Envelope Editor

  3. Define the Start of your loop by entering a sample location in the first window

  4. Define the End of your loop by entering a sample location in the second window

  5. Test the loop by double-clicking its title in the Wavetable list, or playing a note key on the keyboard, in the Pattern Editor.

  6. Get comfortable. Repeat these steps until you find it sounds correct.
