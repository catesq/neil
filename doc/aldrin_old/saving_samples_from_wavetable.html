Saving Samples from Wavetable

In some instances you will want to save a sample from the Song's Wavetable, back to disk. This will happen on one of the two following occasions:

  1. You've downloaded a great song you're just dying to rip samples from.
  2. A machine has added or changed a sample in the Wavetable and you wish to save it for later use.

Follow these steps to save samples:

  1. Select the sample slot from the song's Wavetable
  2. Press the Save button on the toolbar marked with ">>" .
  3. Choose a path and name the file.
