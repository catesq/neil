Muting Machines
To mute a single machine, either single-click the LED in the upper-left corner, or right-click the machine, and select mute from the pop-up menu . Parenthesis will appear around the machine name, and the machine box will darken to show the machine has been muted. A check will also appear next to the word mute in the right-click pop-up menu indicating the new status.
